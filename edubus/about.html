WHO WE ARE?

TheEduBus (www.TheEduBus.com) is a company owned by Interactiv Experiential Pvt Ltd (IEPL)(www.interactiv.in) . TheEduBus is based out of Gurgaon. The organisation has created a platform for all the education partners and is providing the Best In Class education to the consumers in India across geography through their huge network of education channel partners. This company provides a range of products for pre nursery students to college going students and professionals who would want to enhance their skills. Our uniqueness creates huge value for our consumers, for our education partners and for our channel partners.
